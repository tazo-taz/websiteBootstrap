biography of riddler

The Riddler (Edward Nigma) is an enemy of Batman. Created by writer Bill Finger and artist Dick Sprang, he first appeared in 1948, in Detective Comics #140. Like most Batman villains, the Riddler has become a darker, more three-dimensional character in recent years. Whereas he was once portrayed as a playful but sane criminal trickster, he is now the victim of an intense obsessive compulsion. The Riddler is obsessed with riddles, puzzles, and word games. He delights in forewarning police and Batman of his capers by sending them complex clues.
