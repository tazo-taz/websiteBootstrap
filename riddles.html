Riddles

  • I'm so fast you can't see me, though everyone else can see straight through me. I don't stop until the day you die. What am I?

    Answer

    The blink of an eye.

  • I am wet when drying. What am I?

    Answer

    A towel.

  • What is it that is full all day and empty at night?

    Answer

    Shoes.

  • What's something that's easy to see but hard to look at?

    Answer

    The Sun.

  • A pregnant lady named her children: Dominique, Regis, Michelle, Fawn, Sophie and Lara. What will she name her next child? Jessica, Katie, Abby or Tilly?

    Answer

    Tilly. She seems to follow the scale Do, Re, Me, Fa, So, La, and then Ti.

  • The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?

    Answer

    Footsteps.

  • What has a head, a tail, is brown, and has no legs?

    Answer

    A penny.

  • David's father has three sons : Snap, Crackle and _____ ?

    Answer

    David.

  • What has many keys, but can't even open a single door?

    Answer

    A piano.
